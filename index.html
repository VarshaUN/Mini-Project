Explore Mysuru


Experience Mysuru's Cultural Heritage



Popular in Mysuru during October

Chamundi Hills

Rise to divine heights at Chamundi Hill, Mysuru's sacred crown, where ancient temples meet breathtaking vistas. Hike up to the majestic Chamundeshwari Temple, witness stunning sunrise views, and experience spiritual bliss amidst nature's splendor.

Dasara Procession

Experience the vibrant grandeur of Mysuru Dasara! Witness the majestic procession of decked-up elephants, resplendent royal floats, and cultural performances, culminating in a dazzling display of lights, sound, and tradition.
